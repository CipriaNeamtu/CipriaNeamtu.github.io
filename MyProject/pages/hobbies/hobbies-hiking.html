Hike Tours

Practically all my passions were born from my first passion, cycling.

Thanks to my colleagues from the cycling group, I started to take turns in the mountain huts, both in winter and in summer. I visited beautiful places in our country, places about which I had learned so much about geography, but I never had the curiosity to visit them until then.

Because I liked to take photos, I decided to switch from phone to DSLR, so I bought an entry-level camera with which I took pictures almost everywhere I was.

So far I have visited several chalets in the country:

  • Diham
  • Valea Sambetei
  • Malaiesti
  • Piatra Mare
  • Curmatura
  • Cuca
  • Garofita
  • Ciucas
  • Negoiu
  • Piatra Singuratica
  • Suru
  • Dochia

But also rising peaks such as:

  • Ciucas
  • Ocolit
  • Omu
  • Negoiu
  • Toaca
