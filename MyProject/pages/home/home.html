Hi,

On this site you will find especially information about me, my career, my studies, projects and passions.

I`m Ciprian, i`m 29 years old and i currently work in a private company from Brasov as a marketing specialist.

I`m a curious person and I like to look to the future with confidence based on the knowledge gained so far. I`m organized, organization is part of my strengths when i start on a road. I realized that sometimes being organized means wasting a lot of time putting all the details in order, but analyzing the long-term results, I realized that organization is a sure way to evolution and performance.

For several years, I have been wondering if I have chosen the right path in terms of my career. Thus, in 2020 I decided to make a change and I enrolled in one of the courses offered by the Informal School of IT, more precisely, Web Development.

Although I studied a programming language in high school, and even learned some HTML, i can say that it does not compare with the experience and the multitude of information I encountered here. The first interaction with the teachers was very good and I realized that is what I want to do next.

In conclusion, I am glad that I took part in this course, I learned how to lay the foundations of a site, how to structure the site from the very beginning and how to combine as elegantly as possible the three languages HTML, CSS and Java Script.

This is my first individual project, it is made 100% by me and I hope you like it!
